How to get hold of our data?

GrantNav

Our searchable dataset for all of out the grants that are published in 360Giving format. We are using elasticsearch so you can look for terms and keywords in the different field!

You can search it here or download the whole data dump here:

JSON CSV

REDASH

A query editor that allows you to query our data in SQL. We added to Redash our GrantNav dump file for April 2018. All you need to do is to log in with your Google Account.
